# Email Mismatch During Invitation Claiming

## What This Error Means

When claiming a swimmer invitation, you must sign in with the **exact email address** that received the invitation.

## How to Resolve

### Option 1: Sign Out and Use the Invited Email ‚≠ê Recommended

**Best for**: When you have access to the invited email

**Steps**:
1. Click **"Sign Out and Continue"**
2. Create account with the invited email address
3. Return to invitation link
4. Complete the claim

**Time**: 2-3 minutes

---

### Option 2: Request Updated Invitation

**Best for**: When you prefer a different email

**Steps**:
1. Click **"Send Email to I Can Swim"**
2. We'll resend to your preferred email
3. Check inbox for new invitation
4. Claim with preferred email

**Time**: Same day (when we process request)
